About Me

I am an associate attorney at Murphy, Hesse, Toomey & Lehane, LLP, where I practice in the areas of labor and employment law, municipal law, and general civil litigation.

During law school at Boston College, I completed a Judicial Internship at the Massachusetts Superior Court and a Legal Internship at Progress Software's General Counsel's Office. I was also selected to represent my alma mater at the 2023 National Moot Court Competition.

Before law school, I earned a master's degree in accounting and spent three years at PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC) as a tax associate, providing tax provision and tax filing services to public corporations. This background in accounting and tax gives me a uniquely analytical approach to complex legal matters.
